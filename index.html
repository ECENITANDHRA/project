Have you ever thought how wireless charging occurs??

Wireless Charging works on the principle of transmission of power wirelessly from transmitter to receiver circuit through mutual induction between the coils.

Yes! You heard it right.

Transmission of Power wirelessly from one point to the other is the main theme of our project

Transmitter-circuit

It converts DC Source into AC Source using IRZ44-MOSFET.

Transmitter circuit consists of

  1. DC Power supply
  2. LED
  3. Switch
  4. 1kilo ohm resistors
  5. Z44 MOSFET
  6. An inductor coil

Receiver-circuit

It converts AC Source into DC using Rectification and then into 5V DC with the help of LM7805 Voltage regulator.

Receiver circuit consists of

  1. An inductor coil
  2. Rectifier
  3. Capacitor(470uf/35V)
  4. LM7805 Voltage regulator
  5. Micro USB charging port
